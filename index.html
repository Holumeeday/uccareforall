What We Stand On

The Core Pillars
of our work.

Every programme we deliver is built on four foundational pillars — each designed to create measurable, lasting change in the communities we serve.

🛡️
Advocacy & Protection

Breaking the silence on sexual violence through school seminars, global awareness campaigns, and resource guides for survivors.

🩺
Free Healthcare Access

Bringing medical check-ups, blood sugar screenings, vital consultations, and free medications directly to underserved communities.

⚡
Skill Acquisition & Empowerment

Equipping youths and women with self-sustaining skills — from digital marketing and photography to culinary arts and pastry making.

🏗️
Community Infrastructure

Providing critical clean water projects and school outreach support to communities that need it most across Nigeria and Africa.

Who We Are

Not a desk
organisation.

Founded in 2019 by Ofon Umoh — known globally as The Modeling King — UC Careforall Initiative started with zero funding and a team of passionate volunteers. Six years later, we are a multi-programme organisation delivering verified impact across Nigeria and expanding across Africa.

We are rooted in Ebute Metta, Lagos, guided by the principles of safety, equality, and human dignity. We do not give aid and walk away — we build capacity, document every result, and share it with the world.

The King
Walks Africa.

One King. One Country. One Well.

Our most ambitious programme — our founder travels across African countries in full traditional Nigerian royal attire, documenting culture and commissioning one solar-powered water project per country. The entire journey is shared globally across Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, and YouTube.
