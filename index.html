About Us

Rooted in Ebute Metta, Lagos, Nigeria, we are dedicated to driving sustainable social impact across Africa. Guided by the principles of safety, equality, and human dignity, we champion the protection of human rights, advocate for justice, and support vulnerable communities. Alongside our advocacy efforts, we deliver critical healthcare assistance to underserved populations, helping to ensure that access to quality medical care is not limited by geography or economic circumstances. Through these combined efforts, we strive to build healthier, safer, and more inclusive communities for generations to come.

Our Campaigns

At UC Careforall Initiative, we believe meaningful change happens when the message is taken directly to the people. Rather than waiting for communities to come to us, we reach individuals where they live, learn, worship, commute, and gather. Our campaigns have impacted thousands of Nigerians across 25 states through five key outreach channels: door-to-door community engagement, school outreach programs, traffic awareness campaigns, state-to-state volunteer mobilization, and faith-based awareness initiatives. Every campaign is driven by one guiding message: “Be A Better You.” Because when individuals improve themselves, communities grow stronger, and Africa becomes better.

Community Outreach (Door-to-Door Campaign)

Driving community transformation through grassroots engagement and nationwide outreach.

Community Outreach

Our door-to-door community outreach brings awareness and education directly into homes. Field teams engage families and individuals within neighbourhoods, delivering impactful messages on violence prevention, personal development, and community empowerment. No community is too remote, and no household is too small. We meet people where they are to create meaningful, personal connections that inspire change.

School Outreach Program

Empowering communities across Nigeria through awareness, education.

School Outreach Program

At UC Careforall, we believe that transformation begins with education. Our school outreach program delivers awareness, mentorship, and empowerment directly to students across Nigeria. Each visit includes the distribution of essential learning materials such as school bags, books, pens, and educational resources—helping to equip students with the tools they need to learn, grow, and build a brighter future.

State-to-State Volunteer Campaign

Expanding impact through local engagement and nationwide outreach.

State-to-State Volunteer Campaign

This is one of our most ambitious initiatives. We mobilize and train volunteers across 25 states in Nigeria, equipping them with the tools and knowledge to carry the “Be A Better You” message back to their local communities. United by a shared vision, these volunteers form a nationwide movement dedicated to positive change and social transformation.

Traffic Awareness Campaign

Bringing change closer to people, one community at a time.

Traffic Awareness Campaign

Our traffic awareness campaign takes our message to the streets, engaging commuters, motorists, and pedestrians at busy intersections across Nigerian cities. Through direct engagement, we spread awareness on violence prevention, civic responsibility, and community empowerment, reaching thousands of people daily with the “Be A Better You” message.
